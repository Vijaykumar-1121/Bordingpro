How It Works

Generate an optimized boarding sequence in three simple steps.

1

Upload Data

Simply upload your CSV file containing passenger booking IDs and their assigned seats.

2

Process Algorithm

Our smart algorithm analyzes seat positions (like 1A or A1) and groups passengers based on a "Back-to-Front" strategy.

3

Get Sequence

Receive a clear, ordered list that's ready to use, minimizing aisle congestion and boarding time.
